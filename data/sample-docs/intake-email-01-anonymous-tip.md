Subject: Concerning payments via "Adriatic Advisory" — please look into this

To the audit committee,

I am writing anonymously because I am afraid of retaliation. I have worked at
Helios Industrieanlagen for more than seven years in the procurement
department. Over the past 14 months I have noticed a pattern of payments that
I cannot reconcile with our usual procurement processes.

The payments go to a consultancy called **Adriatic Advisory d.o.o.**, based
in Split, Croatia. The total amount paid out across six invoices is roughly
**EUR 740,000**. The invoices reference "market access advisory services for
Western Balkans tenders" but I cannot find any associated deliverables,
status reports, or named consultants in our project files.

Two things stand out:

1. The payments coincide with our successful bids on three public-sector
   heating contracts in Croatia and Bosnia between Q2 and Q4 2025.
2. The contract with Adriatic Advisory was approved outside of the normal
   procurement workflow. It was signed by **Mr. K. Petrović** (Regional
   Sales Director, SEE) without the second signature from Legal that our
   policy requires for engagements above EUR 100,000.

I am sharing what I know because I believe this may violate our internal
anti-bribery policy and possibly the German "Verbandssanktionengesetz"
context as well as local laws in the countries concerned. I will not provide
my identity, but I am happy to answer follow-up questions through this
channel if you reply to this email.
