# One-paragraph intake tip (workshop fixture)

> Paste this whole paragraph at the `You>` prompt when the orchestrator
> asks for the tip text.

Marko Schneider, Deputy Head of Internal Audit at the audited entity,
reports that during a routine sample test he found nine invoices from
Adriatic Advisory d.o.o. (Split, Croatia) booked to account 4710 between
November 2024 and December 2025 with a total volume of approximately
EUR 740,000, all labelled "market access advisory Western Balkans" but
without any deliverables, status reports or named consultants on file.
Every invoice was approved by Krešimir Petrović (Regional Sales Director
SEE) alone, missing the second Legal signature that the group's
anti-bribery policy BR-COM-02 §7 requires for engagements above
EUR 100,000. Over the same period the company won three public-sector
heating contracts in Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina (Zagreb-UKC,
Sarajevo barracks, Split-UKC) totalling roughly EUR 11.0 million, with
success fees of 4% paid to Adriatic on each award. Anna Berger
(Procurement Lead SEE) confirms she never engaged Adriatic and has
never seen any deliverables. Schneider believes this may amount to
improper payments influencing the award decisions and requests a
confidential initial assessment.
