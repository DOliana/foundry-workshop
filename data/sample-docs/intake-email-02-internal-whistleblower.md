Dear Dr. Bauer,

I am writing to you in your capacity as Compliance Officer because for some
time now I have been making observations that I can no longer keep to myself.
I request confidential handling under our whistleblower policy (LkSG-compliant).

**Brief background on me:** Marko Schneider, 11 years with Helios, currently
Deputy Head of Internal Audit.

**Facts as I see them:**

1. As part of a routine sample test I came across six invoices from
   *Adriatic Advisory d.o.o.* (Split, HR) with a total volume of approximately
   EUR 740,000, booking period 11/2024 to 12/2025.
2. The invoices are labelled "market access advisory Western Balkans" without
   our project files containing any deliverables, status reports or
   individually named consultants.
3. The engagement was awarded without the second signature from Legal
   required by group policy. The signatory was Mr. Krešimir Petrović
   (Regional Sales Director SEE).
4. At the same time we won public-sector heating construction contracts in
   Croatia (two university hospitals) and Bosnia and Herzegovina (one
   barracks), with a total volume of approximately EUR 12.4 million.
5. A direct connection between the advisor payments and the contract awards
   is not documented, but the timing is conspicuous.

**Note on possible offence:**
In my view there is an initial suspicion that these may be improper
payments intended to influence award decisions (§ 334 StGB — bribery in
foreign business dealings, in conjunction with IntBestG).
